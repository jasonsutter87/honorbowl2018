At ECHS, we believe that every student can achieve more than they imagined. From our dedicated faculty and staff to our innovative leadership initiatives to our beautiful, state-of-the-art campus, Eastside Catholic's High School program guides each student to achieve his or her God-given potential.

High school is a formative time, so the habits and values established will serve as powerful engines of personal and professional success for the rest of a student's life. At ECHS, students learn the foundational skills to be both great leaders and humble community servants. Students cultivate their talents and passions, and also learn the value of friendship. Welcome to the incredible learning experience that is EC.

Located on 50 acres in Sammamish, Washington, our beautiful state-of-the-art campus facilities, built in 2008, provide an optimum learning environment. Amenities include fully-equipped science labs, integrated classroom technology and dedicated gathering areas for student collaboration. The campus is also home to the St. Francis of Assisi Chapel, a 13,000-volume digital learning library and much more.

Mission:

Eastside Catholic School is a Catholic faith-based educational community where students learn to integrate their thinking and believing in ways that encourage intellectual excellence, nurture relationships and inspire a life of leadership and service to others.
