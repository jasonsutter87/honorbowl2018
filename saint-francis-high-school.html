Founded more than 60 years ago, Saint Francis is rooted in the Catholic, Holy Cross philosophy of educating the whole person, and we place equal importance on the spiritual, intellectual and social development of our students. We are incredibly proud of our Lancers, who chase their passions in all areas of the school mission. From the students who serve the economically poor in local and global communities to the young scientists who solve problems with high-level independent research, from the athletes who win state and national championships to the artists whose work hang in local galleries, students at Saint Francis are constantly supported by a dedicated faculty and staff who help them stretch and grow.

When our students graduate, they are prepared to tackle the social, economic and environmental challenges of their generation with vital skills they developed at Saint Francis, skills such as critical thinking, communication, collaboration and information literacy. Additionally, they become Holy Cross men and women who are people of integrity, respect the dignity of every person, celebrate family and bring hope to others.
