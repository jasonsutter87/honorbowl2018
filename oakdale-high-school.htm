Oakdale High School is a high school located in the Oakdale Joint Unified School District in Oakdale, California, United States. The mascot is the Mustang. The school serves students in grades nine through twelve.

OHS takes PRIDE in becoming lifelong learners. Preparing Responsible Students In a Diverse environment while aiming for Excellence.

OHS ESLR’s Oakdale High Mustanges will be…

  • E&npsp; Engaged learners achieving academic growth
