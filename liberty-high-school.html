Liberty High School is the oldest comprehensive high school located in Brentwood, California, an East Contra Costa County suburb of the San Francisco Bay Area. Opened in 1902, the school is situated in the original downtown area. Liberty serves the communities of Brentwood, Byron, Discovery Bay, Knightsen and Oakley. We are surrounded by farmland and housing developments, reflecting the shift from an agricultural to a suburban community. Liberty currently operates under an A/B block schedule, which allows for depth as well as breadth of learning. The campus embodies the Liberty Union High School District's commitment to personalized student education through den communities providing a common administrator, counselor and secretary throughout grades 9-1
