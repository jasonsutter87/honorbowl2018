Gilroy High School

750 West 10th Street

Gilroy, CA 95020

Mascot: Mustangs

Head Coach: Jacob Newman

2017 Season: 13-0

Pacific Coast – Gabilan League Champions

CIF CC Section Champions

Gilroy High School is the largest school in the Gilroy Unified School District in terms of enrollment and acreage! Enrollment: 1,700 students Mascot: Mustangs School colors: Blue and White

30 million dollars in renovations and modernization over the past five years, which includes:

  • Renovated theater, stadium, gym, campus, and classrooms.
  • New student/staff parking lot with solar paneled covered parking.
  • New two-story, 20 classroom, math building to be completed for the 2018-19 school year.
  • Additional resources giving our students greater access to technology

The athletics program boasts several league titles and Central Coast Section (CCS) titles over the past years. The football team went undefeated (13-0) and won its first CCS title in 2017. Additional Section titles in the sports of boys’ soccer, softball, boy’s track and field, field hockey and wrestling in 2017. The boy’s wrestling team has won 16 consecutive CCS titles and has placed in the top ten in the state for 10 consecutive years including 2nd in 2018.

Remember that every day of the week that ends in the letter “y” is a great day to be a Mustang!
